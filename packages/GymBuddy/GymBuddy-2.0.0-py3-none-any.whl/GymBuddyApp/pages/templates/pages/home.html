Story About Us

We are a group of College Students studying Computer Science at the University of Florida. We all share a strong passion for both mental and physical health/fitness, as well as helping others upgrade and become the best versions of themselves.

In the past, we have all shared similar struggles in finding a coherent application to track various aspects of our health. Therefore, we decided to come together and create an "all in one" application that tracks bodyweight, lifts, macros, and calories, suggest workout plans and caloric intake per day, and more all personalized to the individual!

Macros and Calorie Tracker

  • Here at GymBuddy, you will be able to track Carbohydrates, Fats, Protein, Calories!
  • The dates you enter will be automatically updated to keep you accountable!
  • We also implmented a webscraper to provide a database for you to look up different foods nutritional information without the need for you to input manually. How convenient!
  • You will also be able to better visualize your progress in the form of graphs and charts on the profile tab!

Healthy Meals Database

  • Here at GymBuddy, you will be able to browse our grand selection of various healthy meals to inspire your meal preps!
  • Each meal provides instructions, ingredients, as well as tags for easy filtering!

Lifts Tracker and Strengths Comparison
